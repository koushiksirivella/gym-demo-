DO YOU OFFER A FREE TRIAL? +

Yes! Every new member gets a free 7-day trial with full access to all equipment, classes, and facilities. No credit card required.

WHAT ARE THE GYM HOURS? +

Pro and Elite members get 24/7 access. Starter members can train from 5 AM to 11 PM, 7 days a week.

IS PERSONAL TRAINING INCLUDED? +

Personal training is included in the Elite plan (3 sessions/week). Pro and Starter members can add PT sessions at a discounted rate.

CAN I CANCEL ANYTIME? +

Absolutely. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. You can cancel your membership at any time — we believe in earning your loyalty every month.

DO YOU HAVE PARKING? +

Yes, free dedicated parking for all members with space for 50+ vehicles including two-wheelers and cars.
